About ESMS Project

Project Vision

The Environmental Sustainability Management System (ESMS) is designed to digitize and streamline college administrative processes, reducing paper usage and promoting environmental sustainability through efficient digital solutions. Our goal is to create a paperless campus environment while improving administrative efficiency.
